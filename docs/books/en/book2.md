Clan wealth scores are driven by chest rewards which are opened among the clan. Each chest is worth a score which increases with rarity. One chest earned is awarded to all clan members, meaning that a full clan and active members is crucial for growth. This is why Carter really is so crucial!

Superiors and leaders can claim these gifts every 6.5hrs here:

- Clan Menu
- Wealth
