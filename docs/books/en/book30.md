This is an event that is specifically designed for levelling your hero.

- Earn XP and Valor.
- You can level up your hero.
- You can unlock more of the skill point tree when your hero is lv65.
- Earn skill points for your hero.

#### This event comes with a warning

**Hero leveling:** Be careful when it comes to levelling your hero. They say your hero level should be around half the level of your best captain.

Levelling your hero too much too quickly can cause you to struggle with quests, missions and events and make your progress slow down.

So of course level your hero but bare this in mind everyone.
