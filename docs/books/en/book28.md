#### How and why to send gold tax

#### How do I send gold ingot tax?

- Enter your city.
- Press on your capital.
- Press Tax.
- Drag the icon to the right and send all.

#### Why do I send tax?
Gold ingots are useless to a player. When we send gold ingots as tax they are sent to the kingdom palace. The palace can then use these ingots to purchase bonuses that affect every player kingdom wide.

**Hoarding ingots is hurting yourself, and your friends!**

#### How to view active kingdom bonuses

- Menu
- Bonuses
- Royal

This shows all purchasable bonuses and all active bonuses that your gold ingots pay for.
