#### Summon Mastery event

This event gives you the chance to earn puzzle pieces for a specific captain. Each elite scroll used earns 10 points and each basic scroll earns 1 point.

Collect points to climb up the ladder of your personal quests to earn puzzle pieces.

The captain alternates each event. Here are some tips:

- Save all the scrolls that you earn.
- Wait until the event contains a captain which is of a benefit to you.
- Use your scrolls 10 at a time for better chance of receiving high tier pieces.
- The summons place is located in your city.

Great event for gaining pieces for captains such as Carter, Strorr, Aydae, etc. Keep your eyes peeled in the events tab!
