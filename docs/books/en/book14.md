#### Watchtower

Watchtower is the most valuable tool in the game and to obtain it is quite simple.

- Use real life money to buy it.
- Save your gold in game from rewards and buy 30 days.
- Farm Dragon Mounds.

To farm Dragon Mounds and reach them fast, send one captain carrying spies only. Trust me!

There are ways to obtain the watchtower without money if you apply yourself. In the meantime just ask in clan chat for someone to provide coordinates for what you desire.
