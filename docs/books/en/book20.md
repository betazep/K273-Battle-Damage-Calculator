#### Low on silver?

If you're struggling for silver, don't make the mistake of filling your city with mansions. Keep your city mainly Farmhouses and try the following.

- Scout cities that are outside the coverage of ROE, and attack when profitable.
- Going to sleep? Send all your captains to villages with spearmen.
- Have one captain farming when you are online (Strorr).
- Research Logistics and level up Strorr. Use hero skill points on resource gathering.
- Stacking troops is a great way to save on silver, but also save on speed ups.
- Scout across kingdoms and pillage when profitable.

There are many ways to make silver if you put your mind to it, guys! This is a strategy game so be strategic!
